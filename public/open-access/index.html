Open Access Philosophy:

I am a strong proponent of open access research. In my opinion, scientists should make every effort to make their research openly available online, to facilitate rapid and easy exchange of ideas, especially when the research is conducted in publicly funded institutes. For this reason, all my articles are either available through open access publication platforms, such as AAAI Publications, or when officially published in closed access platforms, open access is possible through arXiv. All versions are accessible through my Google Scholar page. Similarly, all the software produced relating to our research is fully accessible with standard open source license that supports free distribution.
